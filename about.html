about

Hi, I'm Misty! I grew up loving to create, whether in the form of drawing, needlework, or even personal websites with html/css only an 11 year old would write :)

In 2022, I graduated University of Washington with a degree in Informatics! There, i learned a lot about what it means to build accessible and fair technology that serves people well.

After lots of career exploration, I realized web dev is a perfect combination of the things my brain likes to do; solving puzzling coding problems while creating something pleasing to the eye!

I'm excited to bring my perspective in design and code to a team in the future. Currently I'm open to a variety of opportunities (including open source and volunteering for a cause), so feel free to send me a line.

thanks for visiting :)
